# Privacy Policy

**Canny extension for End-users**

Effective date: 2026-05-06
Maintained by: naqtn (<https://github.com/naqtn>)

## Summary

This extension does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any personal
data.

## What this extension does

The extension runs in your browser only when you visit a Canny-powered
feedback site (any `*.canny.io` site, plus `feedback.vrchat.com` as a
known alias). It enhances how you view and work with such sites — for
example, by showing additional fields on each post in the listings,
by marking posts that match criteria you choose, or by optionally
hiding posts that fall outside your filter.

## Data stored on your device

The extension stores the settings and configuration values you set
through its options and popup, using the browser's local storage API
(`chrome.storage.local`). Examples include which display fields to
add to post listings, which posts to mark, the visibility filter mode
in effect, and any author names you provide for filtering or marking.

This data:

- Stays on your device.
- Is not transmitted to the extension developer or to any third party.
- Can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension, or by
  removing the extension's storage from your browser's settings.

The extension does not store account credentials. Beyond the
configuration values described above, no other persistent data is
saved.

## Network requests

To support its display features, the extension may fetch additional
pages from the same Canny site you are already viewing — for instance,
to read fields about each post that are not present in the post
listing itself. These requests:

- Are made from your browser, using your existing session with the
  Canny site.
- Are not sent to the extension developer or to any third party.
- Are functionally equivalent to you opening those pages yourself.

The extension does not contact any other server, including the
developer's. It does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash
reporting, or advertising code.

## Data sharing and selling

The extension does not share or sell any data with or to anyone,
because it does not collect any data to share.

## Future versions

This policy uses general terms to describe the extension's behavior;
the specific examples in each section reflect features available as
of the effective date above. Future versions may add or rename
features, and the principles stated here — no data collection, no
transmission to the extension developer or to any third party, and
local-only storage of your settings — apply to those changes as
well, unless and until this policy is updated.
